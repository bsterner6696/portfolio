I FIXED IT

As I mentioned on two of my project pages, I was having trouble getting the flash video to load for me in my site. As I finished building this site early, I just spent a couple hours working on that problem. And I feel really stupid, the issue had nothing to do with my code, my adblocker was flagging the flash video and keeping it from loading. So that was a big waste of my time. I added a message to the page that only shows when the video doesn't load which tells you to disable your adblock. It's a neat video, so I think it's worth it. I'm just happy I figured it out, that was going to really bug me otherwise.
